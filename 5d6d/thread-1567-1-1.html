\[\dfrac{2511}{13}\approx193.2<3^5\riff n=8\]
$3^4+3^5+3^6=1053$是第$C_7^3$项，$2511-3^7=324=3^4+3^5$，有$C_6^2$
故$k=C_7^3+C_6^2=50$
