其实还是高中题吧
\[\frac{k}{(k+1)!}=\frac{k+1-1}{(k+1)!}=\frac1{k!}-\frac1{(k+1)!}\]
故
\[\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{k}{(k+1)!}=1-\frac1{(n+1)!}\]
