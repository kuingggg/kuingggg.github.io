\begin{align*}
b_{n+1}=\frac1{2-b_n}&\implies b_{n+1}-1=\frac1{2-b_n}-1=\frac{b_n-1}{2-b_n}=\frac{b_n-1}{1-(b_n-1)} \\
& \implies\frac1{b_{n+1}-1}=\frac1{b_n-1}-1.
\end{align*}
