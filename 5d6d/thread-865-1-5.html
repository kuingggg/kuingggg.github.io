[不等式] 古老的难题

Let $a,b,c\geq 0 $with $a+b+c=1, k=1-\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}$ prove that:
\[ \sqrt{a+k(b-c)^{2}}+\sqrt{b+k(c-a)^2}+\sqrt{c+k(a-b)^2}\leq \sqrt{3} \]

(Phan Thanh Nam)
