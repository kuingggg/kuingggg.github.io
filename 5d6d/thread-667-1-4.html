待定系数法也行，先换元 $x^2=t\in[0,1]$，然后待定两个正数 $a$, $b$，有
\begin{align*}
\sqrt{x^2-x^4}+\sqrt{2x^2-x^4}&=\sqrt{t(1-t)}+\sqrt{t(2-t)}\\
&=\sqrt{at\cdot \frac1a(1-t)}+\sqrt{bt\cdot \frac1b(2-t)} \\
& \leqslant \frac12\left( at+\frac1a(1-t)+bt+\frac1b(2-t) \right) \\
& =\frac1{2a}+\frac1b+\left( a-\frac1a+b-\frac1b \right)\frac t2,
\end{align*}
取等条件为
\[\left\{\begin{aligned}
a-\frac1a+b-\frac1b&=0, \\
\frac1a(1-t)&=at, \\
\frac1b(2-t)&=bt,
\end{aligned}\right.\]
解得
\[\left\{\begin{aligned}
a&=\frac{\sqrt2}2, \\
b&=\sqrt2, \\
t&=\frac23,
\end{aligned}\right.\]
……

& \leqslant \sqrt{(x^2+2-x^2)(1-x^2+x^2)} \\
