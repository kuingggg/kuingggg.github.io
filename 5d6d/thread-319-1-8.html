$\sqrt{\dfrac{a^{2}+2bc}{(b-c)^{2}+2bc}}$
假设$a^{2}>(b-c)^{2}$
则$(a+b-c)(a+c-b)>0$
b>c-a,c>b-a
$b^{2}>(c-a)^{2}$  ,$c^{2}>(b-a)^{2}$
以此类推
