$b[n]^2=4(n-1)^2/[n^2(n+1)^2]=4/n^2+16/(n+1)^2-16/n+16/(n+1)
\sum[b[n]^2,{n,2,∞}]=\sum[4/n^2,{n,2,∞}]+\sum[16/(n+1)^2,{n,2,∞}]-16\sum[1/n-1/(n+1),{n,2,∞}]
=4(π^2/6-1)+16(π^2/6-1-1/4)-16(1/2)
=20π^2/6-20-4-8
=10π^2/3-32≈0.8987$
