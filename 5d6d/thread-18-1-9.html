即要证明$x(x^6+4-x^4-2x^3-x^2)+2>0$
而$x^6+x^6+x^6+x^6+2^3+2^3≥12x^4$即$x^6+4≥3x^4$
只要证明$x(2x^4-2x^3-x^2)+2>0$即$2x^5+2>2x^4+x^3$
又$x^5+x^5+x^5+1+1≥5x^3$即$\frac{3}{5}x^5+\frac{2}{5}≥x^3$
只要证明$\frac{7}{5}x^5+\frac{8}{5}≥2x^4$
而$x^5+x^5+x^5+x^5+2^{2.5}≥5\sqrt{2}x^4$即$\frac{8}{5\sqrt{2}}x^5+\frac{8}{5}≥2x^4$
而$\frac{8}{5\sqrt2}<\frac{7}{5}$所以原不等式成立
