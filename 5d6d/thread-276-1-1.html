$f(x)$为$n$阶多项式，且满足$ \displaystyle \int_{0}^{1} x^kf(x) \mathrm{d} x=0 $  $(k=1,2, \cdots ,n)$
求证：\[ \int_{0}^{1} f^2(x) \mathrm{d} x =(n+1)^2 \left( \int_{0}^{1} f(x) \mathrm{d} x \right)^2\]
